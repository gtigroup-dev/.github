## About GTI

**GTI — Global Technology Association of InfoComm** — is an international cooperation platform founded in 2011 by leading global operators including China Mobile, SoftBank, Vodafone, Bharti Airtel, and Sprint, now T-Mobile US.

Over the years, GTI has evolved from a TD-LTE-focused initiative into a global technology collaboration organization. Today, GTI brings together operators, technology partners, research institutions, industry organizations, and ecosystem contributors across the world to promote open collaboration in next-generation information and communication technologies.

GTI is now entering its **3.0 stage**, with a renewed focus on:

* **International technology cooperation**
* **International industry collaboration**
* **International think-tank cooperation**
* **5G-A / 6G and AI convergence**
* **Digital-intelligent transformation across industries**

---

## Mission

GTI aims to connect global innovation resources, strengthen industry consensus, and accelerate the development, deployment, and adoption of next-generation digital infrastructure.

We work to promote practical collaboration across telecom, cloud, AI, industry digitalization, standards, open-source ecosystems, and sustainable technology development.

---

## Focus Areas

### 5G-A and 6G Evolution

Promoting technology research, industry alignment, ecosystem development, and global cooperation around 5G-Advanced and future 6G systems.

### AI-Native Digital Infrastructure

Exploring the integration of AI with telecom networks, cloud platforms, edge computing, and digital infrastructure to support intelligent services and new business models.

### Open Innovation and Open Source

Supporting open collaboration models, developer ecosystems, shared technical resources, and community-driven innovation.

### Industry Digitalization

Advancing ICT solutions for vertical industries including manufacturing, energy, transportation, logistics, public services, and smart cities.

### Global Industry Research

Publishing insights, white papers, reports, solution studies, and industry observations to support informed decision-making and international cooperation.
